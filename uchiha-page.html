Random Facts about the Uchiha Clan!

  1. The village is part of the four clans that are in the Hidden Leaf Village
  2. They have special eyes called the Sharingan that give them special powers unique to each person in the clan
  3. The Sharingan can evolve to the Mangekyou Sharingan when the person experiences intense trauma
  4. The whole clan was killed by one of its own but the killer didn't kill his own brother
  5. The name Uchiha is an alternative way to say "paper fan" this relates to their clan's symbol
  6. The clan's skills were based on Shinto Deities in a Japanese creation myth
  7. The eyes that the Uchiha's have, has the power to control reality for a limited amount of time if they're willing to lose an eye
